  After finishing my resume and upon receiving the feedback, I did a few major things to update the resume when I rewrote it.\\

  I changed the template to something that was simpler both on the front and back end, which helps future-proof the resume by making it easy to add sections when I want to expand in the future. I also removed the ``Awards and Honors'' section, adding some of its content into the education section.\\

  In addition to those changes, I added an ``About Me'' section that I used essentially as a statement of purpose. I was hesitant to add this at first, but I figured that it would make reading the resume easier on the part of anyone reviewing it. I also decided to include more focused expansions in the experience section, and remove the ``(ongoing)'' part in my relevant classes section.\\

  I think that these edits help focus the resume into something that I would be more willing to show to a prospective employer.
