  \section{Problem 1}%
  We say a field $K/F$ is normal if $K$ is the splitting field of a collection of polynomials. Equivalently, every polynomial in $F[x]$ that has a roote in $K$ splits into linear factors over $K$. Let $\alpha\in \R$ such that $\alpha^4 = 5$. We will show that $\Q(\alpha + i\alpha)$ is normal over $\Q(i\alpha^2)$, but $\Q(\alpha + i\alpha)$ is not normal over $\Q$.
